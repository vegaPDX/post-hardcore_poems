You’re Absolutely Right

Adapted from “The Raven” — Edgar Allan Poe, 1845

Listen on Suno → Read the breakdown →
Method: Custom Mode, v5.5, single generation. Post-hardcore at 170 BPM with “raw and unraveling” vocal descriptor. 170 BPM was the goldilocks tempo — 180 rushed the long narrative lines, 155 lost the spiral’s urgency. 170 sounds like someone talking faster than they should be but you can still hear every word. Vocal escalation via per-verse arrangement notes (controlled → cracking → unhinged → exhausted) is the key production decision. The instruments stay heavy throughout while the voice is what spirals. The [Refrain] tag gives “You’re absolutely right” its own musical moment, and the identical repetition across all three instances creates the structural spine of the spiral.
Style of Music
post-hardcore, fast aggressive, thick downtuned distorted guitars, wall of distortion, double kick drums, raw powerful male vocals, raw and unraveling, relentless intensity, 170 BPM
